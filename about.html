About OrderEase

OrderEase is Dehradun's smart food ordering system, designed to connect citizens with their favorite local restaurants in the most convenient and eco-friendly way possible. Our mission is to make city life easier, tastier, and more sustainable by leveraging technology and community partnerships.

Our Story

Founded in 2023, OrderEase was born out of a passion for food and a vision for a smarter city. We noticed the need for a seamless, local-first food ordering experience that supports both customers and small businesses. Today, we proudly serve Dehradun with a platform that is fast, secure, and user-friendly.

Our Values

  • Customer-first approach
  • Support for local restaurants
  • Eco-friendly delivery solutions
  • Innovation and technology
  • Transparency and trust
